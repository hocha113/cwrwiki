Briny Baron

Sea Lord Sword is a broadsword weapon in Hard mode, dropped from the Arowana Duke, it can be left and long press automatically use
It has an attack cycle of three stages
The first stage is divided into six swings, the blade deals 1 times the weapon panel damage, and each swing launches a sea vortex that tracks the enemy.
The second stage is a wide slash, the blade deals twice the damage of the weapon panel and sends a large number of scattered exploding bubbles around.
In the third stage, the blade points in the direction of the mouse and fires three vortex shells, which explode when they touch objects or hit enemies, creating a water spout in place that will soon disappear.
Its best prefix is legend

Vortex rounds have 1.5 times the weapon panel damage, but their contact explosion does no actual range damage, don't let the particle effect of the explosion fool you.
The Whirlpool bullet inflicts half as much damage as the waterspout, which is 0.75 times the weapon panel damage, dealing dampness to enemies it touches

——The Sea Lord Sword was the first weapon to be modified in the Calamity Overhaul Mod, and it has undergone two reworks
