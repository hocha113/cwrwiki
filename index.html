What is Terraria Origins?

Terraria Origins is a fairly-sized content mod that enriches the vanilla Terraria experience with similar gameplay balance and likeness. Origins adds content spanning all of Terraria's progression with a small portion after defeating the Moon Lord. The main features of Origins include new enemies, biomes, an explosive-based Demolitionist class, songs for all of the content, and more that you can find on this wiki.

Thank you for showing interest in our project and we hope you have had a lot of fun with it!

***If you have questions about the wiki, it would be best to discuss it on the official discord found in the link above!
